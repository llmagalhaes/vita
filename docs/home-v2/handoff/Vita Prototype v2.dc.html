Screens
Try in the demo
  • Sign in: Google/Apple show a consent step · email sends a magic link — “Open the link” lands on onboarding
  • Trends: tap any card to expand it — then drag across the chart to read each day
  • Tweaks: set “menu” to v2 — tap the mic pill to unfold the text field; hold it to speak
  • Home: tap the cards — details unfold beneath · Tweaks “home” v2: a Plan gauge replaces Water, Today becomes a timeline that expands in place — drag across the dot strip (dock-style magnifier) or swipe the timeline to browse the last 10 days
  • Account (person icon on Home): profile card expands to edit · “Export to…” shapes a PDF per reader
  • Camera button (input bar, or inside the v2 Log pill): a photo becomes an editable meal or routine — steppers, remove, or discard all
  • Account → Vacation mode: pick notifications to keep, add “Beach walk” by voice, set dates — the app shifts to a sea tone; Trends can hide those days
  • Home: tap the check-ins banner — answer cards advance; drag one down to dismiss
  • Swipe left/right on the phone: Home → Trends → Workout → Habits → Integrations
  • Home: tap the mic, watch the parse, then Confirm the snack card
  • Habits: 13:00 plan check-in — “Yes” logs plan lunch; “Not quite” opens capture · Manage tab edits habits
  • Workout: tap a History row — preview rises; drag it down to close
  • Home: tap the Macros card for today’s meals · Eating plan row opens the plan
  • Trends → Activity: tap a muscle — exercises rise in a sheet; tap one to expand
  • Meal plan: tap an item, drag the slider — totals update behind the blur
Notes
  • “Vita” — life; sounds the same in PT and EN
  • No goals, scores or advice anywhere — copy stays factual
  • Dual input throughout: every tappable answer can be spoken or typed, and vice versa
  • Platform-agnostic chrome · sample data for “Ana”
