# Fable design-fidelity audit — prototype vs app

**Date:** 2026-07-14 · **Prototype:** `docs/prototype/vita-prototype.dc.html` · **App:** `app/services/vita-app/src` + `app/services/vita-app/app`
**Scope:** look & motion fidelity only. Read-only audit; no code changed.

---

## 1. Verdict

The app is **structurally faithful and motionally flat**. Layout, palette, typography (Nunito 200–800), copy tone, per-kind wave illustrations, the capture pill unfold, and the three drag-dismiss sheets (capture / check-in / review) all match the prototype closely — an implementer squinting at stills would call it ~85% there. What's missing is almost entirely the prototype's **motion layer**: (1) **nothing responds to touch** — every prototype button/card compresses on press (`style-active: scale(.94–.985)`), the app's Pressables change nothing or only opacity; (2) **data never draws itself** — the prototype grows every chart bar (`vtGrowY`), draws every curve (`vtDraw`), sweeps every donut arc (`vtArc`) and slides every progress bar (`transition: width .4–.6s`); the app renders all of them at final state; (3) **state changes snap** — expand/collapse sections, chevrons, toggles, sheet content swaps and pop-ups appear instantly where the prototype fades (`vtFade`), pops (`vtPop` on a `.2,.8,.3,1` bezier), rotates or springs. A distant fourth theme: four sheets/pop-ups still use stock RN `Modal` (`slide`/`fade`) instead of the app's own `useSheetDrag` + `SlideInDown` pattern, and app `Card` has **no shadow** while every prototype card floats on `0 10px 26px rgba(105,84,60,.08)` — the single cheapest "feels flat" fix in the codebase. The good news: the motion tokens (`src/ui/tokens.ts:46-50`), reanimated 4, and one exemplary primitive (`WaveIllustration` — a correct `vtDraw` port) already exist; the backlog below is mostly "apply the pattern everywhere," not new architecture.

**Top 3 divergence themes:** ① press/touch feedback + micro-transitions (chevrons, toggles, expands), ② chart draw-on animations (`vtGrowY`/`vtDraw`/`vtArc`/bar-width), ③ sheet & pop-up choreography (rise + drag-dismiss + `vtPop`, stacked check-in cards).
